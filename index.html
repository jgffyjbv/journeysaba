What Is ABA Therapy?

Science-Backed. Child-Centered. Life-Changing.

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) is the gold-standard, evidence-based therapy for children with autism spectrum disorder (ASD) and other developmental differences. ABA uses proven techniques to teach communication, social, and daily living skills — while reducing behaviors that interfere with learning and quality of life.

Evidence-Based

ABA is endorsed by the US Surgeon General and the American Psychological Association as an effective treatment for autism.

Individualized Plans

Every child receives a custom treatment plan designed by a Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) based on their unique needs and goals.

Family Partnership

We work closely with families and caregivers, providing training and support so progress continues beyond therapy sessions.

How to Get Started with Journeys ABA

We make the process simple and supportive from day one.

Free Consultation

A BCBA conducts a comprehensive assessment to understand your child's strengths and areas for growth.

03

We develop an individualized treatment plan with clear, measurable goals tailored to your child.

Therapy Begins

Your child starts sessions with a dedicated RBT, supervised by a BCBA, with regular progress reviews.
