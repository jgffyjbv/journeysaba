What We Offer

Our ABA Therapy Programs

We meet your child where they are — at home, at school, or in the community.

In-Home ABA Therapy

Learning in the comfort of your home

Who This Serves
Individuals ages 2–21 diagnosed with ASD

Our in-home ABA therapy program brings evidence-based treatment directly to your family. Children learn best in familiar environments — our BCBAs and RBTs work with your child in the natural setting where skills are most meaningful and generalization happens naturally.

Individualized treatment plans designed by a BCBA
Natural environment teaching (NET)
Family training and caregiver coaching included
Flexible morning, afternoon, and evening scheduling
Regular progress reviews and plan updates
Coordination with school and other providers
Ask about this service

School-Based Support

Helping children succeed in the classroom

Who This Serves
Individuals ages 2–21 diagnosed with ASD

Our school-based ABA services support children in educational settings by collaborating with teachers, aides, and school staff. We help develop strategies that promote learning, reduce challenging behaviors, and build the social skills needed to thrive alongside peers.

School behavior support planning
Classroom behavior support strategies
Social skills training with peers
Staff and teacher consultation and training
Transition planning support
Data collection and progress monitoring
Ask about this service

Community-Based Therapy

Building independence in the real world

Who This Serves
Individuals ages 2–21 diagnosed with ASD

Community-based ABA therapy helps children generalize skills beyond the home and classroom — in grocery stores, parks, restaurants, and other real-world environments. We focus on building independence, safety awareness, and social participation in everyday life.

Real-world skill generalization
Community safety and independence training
Social integration and peer interaction
Functional communication in public settings
Adaptive behavior and self-care skills
Transition to adulthood support

Individuals ages 2–21 diagnosed with ASD

We believe parents and caregivers are the most important people in a child's life. Our caregiver training program equips families with the ABA strategies, tools, and confidence to support their child's growth 24/7 — not just during therapy hours.

Hands-on ABA strategy training

Getting Started

How to Get Started

We make the intake process simple, supportive, and stress-free.

01

Free Consultation

Call or email us to discuss your child's needs. We'll answer your questions and explain the process.

02

Insurance Verification

We verify your insurance benefits and walk you through coverage so there are no surprises.

03

BCBA Assessment

A Board Certified Behavior Analyst conducts a comprehensive evaluation of your child's skills and needs.

04

Therapy Begins

Your child is matched with a dedicated RBT and therapy starts with clear, measurable goals.
